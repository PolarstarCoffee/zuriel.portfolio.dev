I’m Octavio Zuriel Zavala, and I’m a ludicrously huge fan of the realms of game development and tech. During my time at DePaul University, I had studied game design for my bachelor’s with my minor in computer science. Amidst my studies I grew to love the process of executing an idea or concept through programming. I will never forget the late nights of debugging and scouring through documentation to eventually lead to the eureka moment in which everything falls into its correct place. Combining that with developing alongside an even more passionate team only solidified my love for the field, the people, and their craft.

Now that I’ve graduated, I look forward to the next opportunity in which I can contribute my passion and technical skills to bring to life more wonderful and strange digital experiences. While always trying my best to remain frontwards facing
