The inital brainstorming for this project was quite sporatic from what I remember. The team had many ideas, but nothing was sticking. To get into a creative headspace I had put together a sample scene with a player controller and had urged the team to play wihin the space and note down anything that would arise from their time in engine.

Eventually as development continued we were able to iterate on some core ideas! During such I contributed by implementating the core character controller alongside it's animations. Alongside some supplemental work regarding UI functionality, scene transitions, and XInput controller support!

Take a look at the project on Itch.io! Or alternatively if you want to poke around in-engine, check it out on my Github!
